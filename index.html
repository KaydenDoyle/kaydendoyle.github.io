Kayden Doyle

Indie‑Rock Artist from New York

Kayden Doyle is a dynamic and versatile musician from Long Island, NY—drummer, songwriter, and multi‑instrumentalist. He began drumming in 3rd grade and is now releasing his own music, blending rock, jazz, pop, and introspective lyrics. Kayden has collaborated with bands like Cue The Pilot and Chio, and performed at venues like The Cutting Room in NYC.
